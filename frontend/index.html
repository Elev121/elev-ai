About

Built for Serious Researchers

ELEV is an academic research intelligence platform that combines large-scale paper indexing, natural language processing, and structured quality evaluation to support every stage of the research process.

Multi-Dimensional Scoring

Evaluates research across five core dimensions: semantic coherence, citation quality, methodology rigor, originality index, and results clarity.

Novelty Detection

Cross-references your research problem against 200M+ indexed papers using semantic similarity — not just keyword matching — to surface genuinely unexplored angles.

Reproducible Results

Algorithmic consistency ensures identical submissions receive identical scores, eliminating the reviewer bias that affects traditional manual processes.

Institutional Scale

Supports bulk processing for universities and research bodies, with reporting tools for tracking departmental quality trends over time.
